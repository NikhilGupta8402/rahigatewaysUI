Introduction

At Rahi Travel, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines the ways in which we collect, process, store, and protect your personal information whenever you access our website, mobile application, or any of our travel-related services. Our goal is to provide transparency and empower you to make informed choices about your data.

By using our platform, you explicitly agree to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, we kindly ask that you refrain from using our services. Your continued use of our platform signifies your acceptance and consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information as outlined here.

This policy applies to all individuals interacting with our services, including casual visitors browsing our website, registered users creating accounts, and customers making travel bookings. It also applies to any personal information shared through third-party services integrated with our platform, ensuring that your data is consistently protected across all touchpoints.

We are committed to adhering to applicable data protection regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and India’s DPDP Act. This policy also explains your rights regarding personal information and how you can exercise them, reflecting our dedication to responsible data management and transparency.

Data Security

We follow strict administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to keep your personal information secure. Our security systems are designed to detect threats, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Our Security Measures

  • Encrypted Communication: All information exchanged with our platform is protected using advanced SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive information is stored using industry-standard algorithms designed to prevent misuse.
  • Controlled Access: Only authorized and trained personnel can access limited data based on role, responsibility, and necessity.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Real-time monitoring systems help detect suspicious activity, security threats, and vulnerabilities.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct routine assessments, penetration tests, and compliance checks to maintain high security standards.
  • Compliance with Global Standards: Our practices align with globally accepted security frameworks and data-protection guidelines.
